genetically modified organism Also found in: Medical, Acronyms, Encyclopedia, Wikipedia.Related to genetically modified organism: Genetically modified food
genetically modified organismn. Abbr. GMO An organism whose genetic characteristics have been altered by the insertion of a modified gene or a gene from another organism using the techniques of genetic engineering.
